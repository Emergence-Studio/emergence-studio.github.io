Why Community?

‚ÄúThere is no power for change greater than a community discovering what it cares about.‚Äù ‚Äì Margaret J. Wheatley

Community building is an art. Every business has a unique mission, journey and set of technologies.

After many years building world-class software products to the best companies in the world, I looked at the community building toolkits available and realized how poor the existing tools are. Community builders and members require better "social infrastructure" in order to:

  • Carry conversations successfully from in-person to digital environments
  • Provide members the necessary tools and onboarding required to make them effective community members
  • Enable community managers to scale their communities without sacrificing the quality of the experience
  • Provide community managers with the data and insights required to make informed decisions
  • Enable community managers to connect the feedback and insights back into the broader business

    About.

    Emergence Studio is a platform that enables community builders to leverage the power of data to drive growth and engagement. We do this by providing a suite of tools that enable community builders to create, manage and scale their communities.

    Jameson Lee

    Founder

    Previous to founding Emergence Studio, I have served organizations (both large and small) in a variety of strategic capacities. Most recently, I was a strategic solution architect at Moveworks where I designed, scaled and implemented enterprise-wide, conversational AI platforms. I began my career at Goldman Sachs, and over the past 10 years have applied my understanding of data science, software engineering and sales to develop a solution for a space that is deeply meaningful to me: community building.

    It has been the experiences and connections of the past decade that have taught me the value of community... and also the driving inspiration(s) for how I've developed the playbook for community-driven-growth. My mission is simple: to bring world-class, flexible toolkits to community builders so that they can focus on fostering and transferring those in-person interactions into a digital environment where they can continue the conversation.

    When I'm not working on Emergence, you can find me working on my yoga practice, playing tennis, traveling and writing (mostly about science fiction and AI). I am based out of New York City.
