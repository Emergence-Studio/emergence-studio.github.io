Create Your Emergent Language.

The Philosophy.

Data is software's natural by-product. However, learning to use data is slow, expensive and requires specialized expertise in order to access its value.

Teams looking to take on these problems need repeatable, easy to interpret solutions.

Emergence Studios was created to define and provide these solutions: to offer teams the ability to leverage the experience of those who have answered these questions in the past, and create playbooks to ease the burden of operationalizing value evangelism, analytic enablement, and providing the insights - both to customers and internal teams - that data can offer.

Services.

Data is now at the core of every corporate transformation - whether big or small.

Putting data at the center of your organization requires developing a language that allows others to universally understand value. Below is an overview of the steps required to create your own language.

  • Target Account Scoring
  • Use your sales's organizations hard won account / technical discovery data to understand (1) who loves your product, and (2) where to direct efforts in the future based on TAM, SAM, SOM objectives. Assess the quality of your account data, and deliver target accounts to Sales Execs that matter.
  • Defining the Technical Sale
  • Create a data-driven approach to proving value with data from your product. Define value while also differentiating from competitors. Provide your Presales / Post-sales resources the plays they need to drive effective upsell and cross-sell campaigns.
  • Scaling the Technical Sale
  • Scale your technical evaluation to produce the data-driven playbook that provides proof (without the POC). Collecting data from across multiple customers, this project seeks to produce the enablement material that unlocks value at scale across customer segments, verticals, and geographies.
  • Value Quantification & Realization
  • Create a unifying framework to establish economic alignment between your economic buyer, champion, and technical stakeholders. Establish the presales and post-sales motions responsible for achieving technical and business sign-off.
